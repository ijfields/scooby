# Scooby

**"Canva for Stories"**

Turn your written stories into stunning vertical drama videos — no tech skills needed.

---

## The Problem

Writers have amazing stories. But turning them into video content today requires:

- Learning AI tools (ChatGPT, Midjourney, Veo)
- Learning video editing software (CapCut, Premiere)
- Learning voice-over tools (ElevenLabs)
- Hours of manual work per episode

> **Most writers give up before they start.**

---

## The Solution

**Scooby** handles the entire production pipeline.

The writer **only** does what they're good at — **writing**.

Everything else is automated behind a simple, guided interface.

---

## How It Works

### 5 simple steps:

1. **Paste your story** (300-800 words)
2. **Review scene cards** — AI breaks your story into 5-7 visual beats
3. **Pick a style** — choose a visual look and narrator voice
4. **Generate** — AI creates images, voiceover, and assembles the video
5. **Download** — get your finished MP4, ready for TikTok/Reels/Shorts

---

## What the Writer Sees

| Step | What Happens | Writer's Job |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| Story Intake | Paste or type the story | Write! |
| Scene Cards | AI breaks story into beats | Review & tweak |
| Style Selection | Choose visual style + voice | Pick what feels right |
| Generation | AI makes images, audio, video | Wait ~3 minutes |
| Export | Download finished video | Share it! |

---

## The Output

A **60-90 second vertical drama video** (9:16 format):

- Professional AI-generated scene images
- Narrated voiceover synced to each scene
- Animated captions/subtitles
- Background music matching the mood
- **1080x1920 MP4** ready for posting

Perfect for **TikTok**, **Instagram Reels**, and **YouTube Shorts**.

---

## The Tech (Simplified)

You don't need to know the details, but here's the gist:

- **Website:** Built with modern web tools (Next.js)
- **AI Brain:** Claude analyzes stories into scenes
- **AI Eyes:** Stability AI creates the scene images
- **AI Voice:** ElevenLabs generates the narration
- **Video Engine:** Remotion assembles everything into a video

All of this happens **automatically** behind the scenes.
