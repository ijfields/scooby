## Your questions, answered

- * * *

- Can I export videos without a watermark?






Yes! Even if you have a free Canva account, you can export videos without a watermark as long as you use free elements, images, footage, and music. If you’re on Canva Pro, your videos won’t have any watermarks whether you’re using free or paid elements.



Watermarks will only appear if you use a Canva Free account and add Pro or paid elements to your videos.

- * * *

- Are captions supported?






Yes, you can automatically [generate captions](https://www.canva.com/features/add-subtitles-to-video/) with Canva’s video editor. Just upload the video clip to which you want to add captions, select **Text** on the editor side panel, then select **Dynamic text** \> **Captions** \> **Generate captions** to auto-generate your subtitles.

- * * *

- Can I export my video in various formats and aspect ratios?






Yes, you can export your video as an MP4 or GIF. As you edit your video, use [Magic Resize (Pro)](https://www.canva.com/pro/magic-switch/) to change the aspect ratio and resize it for different platforms.

- * * *

- How easy is it to publish directly from Canva to social platforms?






You can publish your videos directly from Canva to social platforms like Facebook, Instagram, X (formerly Twitter), TikTok, Pinterest, LinkedIn, and Tumblr. To do this, click **Share** on the top right corner above the editor, select **See all** and scroll down to **Social**, then select the app you want to use. If this is your first time using the app with Canva, you’ll have to connect your account first and give the app access to Canva.



If your file fails to share to your preferred platform, check our guide to [social media upload limitations](https://www.canva.com/help/sharing-social-media-limitations/?query=publish%20videos%20to%20social%20media) to see if it meets the supported file types, sizes, and conditions.

- * * *

- Does Canva’s video editor integrate with cloud storage?






Yes, Canva’s video editor integrates with different cloud storage and file-sharing apps. To choose an upload option, go to **Uploads** on the editor side panel and select the three dots beside the **Upload files** button. You can also search our **Apps** to connect your preferred cloud storage account to Canva.

- * * *

- Does Canva’s video editor offer AI-powered editing?






Yes, Canva’s video editor has AI-powered tools like **Highlights**, which identifies your videos’ best moments in seconds, **Enhance Voice**, which removes background noise from voice recordings, and **Magic Video**, which creates social-ready clips in seconds by styling, cutting, and sequencing clips for you. You can also generate videos with a simple text prompt using **Magic Media**.

- * * *

- Can I edit videos on my phone with Canva?






Yes, you can edit videos on Canva’s free [mobile video editing app](https://www.canva.com/video-editor/mobile-app/). Just download Canva for mobile via the App Store or Google Play to get started.

- * * *

- Does Canva take any ownership of my content or use it to train AI?






No, we don’t claim ownership of your content. We don’t repurpose it, license it, or use it without your permission. Canva is built with creators at its heart.



In addition, we're committed to never training, selling, or repurposing your content without your explicit permission. Our proactive consent model means you can opt out of having your designs used to train our AI models at any time.



If you design something in Canva, it’s yours. Not ours. Not anyone else’s.
